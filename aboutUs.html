Martomix Network Story

The name of the Company is: MARTOMIX COMMUNICATIONS AND CONSULTANCY LIMITED. The company was established in November 2009 and registered as a limited company in 2015. The vision of the company is to become the leading Internet Service Provider within Nairobi and its environment. The objectives of the company are to provide residential and business internet services including the provision of broadband communication services and internet access solutions. Besides, the provision of internet services the company is involved in the network designs, installation of CCTV and alarm systems, testing and commissioning of all types of communications and telecommunications equipment and systems, media transmission and receiving equipment, servicing and repair of computers of all types of computers and computer systems and carrying out business as consultants and advisers in respect of the mentioned services. Our product portfolio serves multiple applications within four primary end markets: wired infrastructure, wireless communications, enterprise storage and industrial & others. Applications for our products in these end markets include: data center networking, home connectivity, broadband access, telecommunications equipment, smartphones and base stations, data center servers and storage, factory automation, power generation and alternative energy systems, and displays. Martomix Communications and Consultancy Limited combines global scale, engineering depth, broad product portfolio diversity and superior execution and operational focus to deliver category-leading connectivity products so its customers can build and grow successful businesses today and in the future.
